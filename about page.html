About Us

Welcome to Interior Dekho, your ultimate destination for transforming your living space into a haven of elegance, comfort, and sophistication. Our platform allows you to upload and showcase your interior design ideas and products, making it easier for others to find inspiration and shop for the same items.

At Interior Dekho, we believe that every home should be a reflection of your unique style and personality. Our mission is to create a seamless experience for users to discover, share, and shop for beautiful home decor.

Whether you are an interior designer looking to showcase your projects or a homeowner eager to share your beautiful living space, our platform is designed to cater to your needs. By uploading your interior pictures, you not only inspire others but also have the opportunity to earn from your photos.

Each picture you upload includes a link or location of where the product was bought, enabling viewers to purchase the product directly without any hassle. Join us on this journey to make every home a masterpiece.
